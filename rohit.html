THE HITMAN

Rohit Gurunath Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, is one of the most destructive batsmen in modern cricket. From being a talented youngster to becoming one of the most successful captains in limited-overs cricket, Rohit's journey is filled with record-breaking performances.

Known for his elegant batting and ability to score big hundreds, Rohit made his international debut in 2007. His transformation from a middle-order batsman to a world-class opener in 2013 changed the course of his career, leading to numerous records.

Rohit took over limited-overs captaincy from Virat Kohli in 2021 and has since led India to several memorable victories. Under his leadership, India reached the finals of the 2023 World Test Championship and the 2023 ODI World Cup.

Beyond his international success, Rohit has been the most successful captain in IPL history, leading Mumbai Indians to 5 titles. He holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs (264) and is the only player with three double-centuries in ODI cricket.

THE KOHLI SAGA

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, is one of the greatest batsmen in modern cricket history. From leading India to numerous victories to rewriting record books, Kohli's journey is a testament to his dedication and passion for the game.

Known for his aggressive batting and fitness revolution, Kohli made his international debut in 2008 and quickly established himself as a key player. His first ODI century came in 2009 against Sri Lanka, and there was no looking back.

Kohli took over Test captaincy in 2014 and became full-time limited-overs captain in 2017. Under his leadership, India became a dominant force across formats, achieving historic Test series wins abroad and reaching the No. 1 ranking in all formats.

Beyond his international success, Kohli has been the face of Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and is regarded as one of the greatest chasers in limited-overs cricket with numerous match-winning innings.

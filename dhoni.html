THE DHONI STORY

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), is one of the most successful captains in Indian cricket history. From being a ticket collector to becoming the most loved cricketer in India, Dhoni's journey is nothing short of inspirational.

Known for his calm demeanor and sharp cricketing mind, Dhoni made his international debut in 2004 and quickly rose through the ranks with his explosive batting and lightning-fast wicketkeeping. His 148 against Pakistan in 2005 announced his arrival on the international stage.

Dhoni took over the captaincy in 2007 and led India to victory in the inaugural T20 World Cup. Under his leadership, India reached the No. 1 ranking in Test cricket, won the 2011 ODI World Cup after 28 years, and the 2013 Champions Trophy, completing a unique treble of ICC trophies.

Beyond his international success, Dhoni has been the face of Chennai Super Kings in the IPL, leading them to multiple titles and creating a legacy as one of the greatest finishers in limited-overs cricket.
